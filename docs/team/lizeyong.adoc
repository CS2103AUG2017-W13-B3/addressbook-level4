== Project: ABC Business Contact
*ABC Business Contact* is a free desktop contact management application that helps users to manage their large collection of contacts conveniently and keep track of their appointments. Users can store contacts as well as other information and retrieve them efficiently with the help of *ABC*. +
 Users interact with it using a Command Line Interface(CLI), and it has a GUI created with JavaFX. It is written in Java, and has about 6 kLoC.
